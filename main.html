Candle Formula

Learn how to create our candles!

  • Materials
    • 1 part soy wax
    • 1 part fragrance oil (optional, for scented candles)
    • Candle wick
    • Container or mold for the candle
    • Wick holder
Candle Instructions

Learn how to create our candles!

  • Instructions
    • 1. Prepare the Container: If you're using a container for your candle, make sure it's clean and dry. If you're using a mold, prepare it according to its instructions.
    • 2. Melt the Wax: In a double boiler or a heat-safe container placed in a pot of water, melt the wax over low to medium heat. Be sure to monitor the temperature to avoid overheating.
    • 3. Add Fragrance (Optional): If you want a scented candle, add fragrance oil to the melted wax. The amount of fragrance oil depends on your preference and the type of wax you're using. Follow the manufacturer's guidelines for safe usage.
    • 4. Prepare the Wick: While the wax is melting, prepare the wick. Attach one end of the wick to the bottom center of the container using a wick holder or by dipping it in the melted wax and sticking it to the bottom. Make sure the wick is straight.
    • 5. Pour the Wax: Once the wax is completely melted and the fragrance oil is mixed in (if using), carefully pour it into the container or mold. Leave some space at the top if you're using a container, as the wax may shrink slightly as it cools.
    • 6. Secure the Wick: As the wax starts to cool and solidify, make sure the wick remains centered. You can use a wick holder or a pencil laid across the top of the container to keep the wick in place.
    • 7. Cool and Trim: Allow the candle to cool and harden completely. This may take several hours depending on the size of the candle. Once the candle is fully cooled, trim the wick to about Â¼ inch (0.6 cm) above the surface of the wax.
    • Enjoy: Your homemade candle is now ready to light and enjoy!
